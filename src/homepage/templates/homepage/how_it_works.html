Who are the Enverest Experts?

Enverest invites an exclusive group of business leaders and domain experts. Most of our experts have more than 10 years of successful experience in their own field, and hold the title of VP and above

How do I pay for the services?

You pay the service fees directly to Enverest, and Enverest will pay the experts less a 25% service fee.

Can I hire the Experts to work for me full time?

Yes, you can. However, if your first contact with the expert is made through Enverest, you have to pay Enverest a finders fee equaling a two-month salary of the full time position. As an alternative, Enverest offers you an options to request the Expert’s service on a recurring basis, without the cost associated with a full time employee.
