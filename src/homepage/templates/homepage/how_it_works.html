{% trans "When someone tries to enter a locked room, they will see a screen saying the room is locked, asking them to take a photo of themselves and knock on the room. {% trans "If there are followers to a room, followers will still receive notifications about chat messages while the room is locked and be able to read this the next time they enter the room." %}
